Our Comprehensive Neuro Rehabilitation Services

Explore the range of specialized therapies we offer to support your recovery and well-being.

Hand Therapy

Specialized therapy focusing on the rehabilitation of the hand and upper limb. Our expert therapists address conditions resulting from injury, surgery, arthritis, and repetitive strain. We provide customized treatment plans to improve strength, range of motion, and fine motor skills, enabling you to return to daily activities and work.

Occupational Therapy

Our occupational therapy services aim to help individuals participate fully in the activities of daily life. We address physical, emotional, and social barriers through comprehensive evaluations and tailored interventions. Our therapists focus on task modification, adaptive equipment training, and environmental adaptations to enhance independence and quality of life.

Stroke Rehabilitation

A comprehensive program designed to aid recovery from stroke. Our multidisciplinary team provides physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy to address motor skills, mobility, communication, and cognitive function. We create individualized rehabilitation plans to optimize recovery and promote independence.

Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapy services focus on restoring movement and function through exercise, manual therapy, and education. We treat a wide range of conditions, including musculoskeletal injuries, neurological disorders, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Our evidence-based approach aims to alleviate pain, improve mobility, and enhance overall physical function.

Splinting and Adaptive Aids

We provide custom splinting and a variety of adaptive aids to support rehabilitation and improve functional independence. Our team specializes in creating orthotic devices to immobilize or support injured joints and offers training in the use of adaptive equipment to make daily tasks easier.

Pressure Garment and Scar Management

Our specialized program focuses on the management of scars and the prevention of complications following injury or surgery. We offer pressure garments and educate patients on effective scar management techniques, including massage and silicone therapy, to optimize healing and improve skin appearance and function.
