"Helps you make it happen"

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate neuro rehabilitation services. Our team of experts is committed to helping you regain function, independence, and improve your quality of life.

Our Services

Hand Therapy

Specialized therapy for conditions affecting the hand and upper limb, including rehabilitation after injury or surgery, arthritis management, and treatment of repetitive stress injuries. We offer customized treatment plans that may include exercises, splinting, manual therapy, and patient education. Our therapists work with patients to improve their strength, range of motion, and fine motor skills, facilitating a return to daily activities and work.

Occupational Therapy

Therapy focused on helping individuals participate in the activities of daily life (ADL), training and home-based care services. Our occupational therapists address barriers that affect a person's emotional, social, and physical needs. We provide comprehensive evaluations, develop individualized treatment plans, and offer interventions such as task modification, adaptive equipment training, and environmental adaptations to support clients in achieving their goals.

Stroke Rehabilitation

Comprehensive program to help individuals recover from stroke, including physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy. Our stroke rehabilitation program is tailored to address each patient's unique needs and challenges. We focus on improving motor skills, mobility, communication, and cognitive function. Our team works collaboratively to provide a range of therapies, including gait training, constraint-induced movement therapy, and speech-language therapy, to optimize recovery and promote independence.

Physiotherapy

Therapeutic approach to restore movement and function through exercise, manual therapy, and education. Our physiotherapists assess and treat a wide range of conditions, including musculoskeletal injuries, neurological disorders, and post-surgical rehabilitation. We utilize evidence-based techniques such as therapeutic exercises, modalities (e.g., ultrasound, electrical stimulation), and manual therapy to alleviate pain, improve mobility, and enhance overall physical function.

Splinting and Adaptive Aids

Custom splinting and adaptive aids to support rehabilitation and improve function. Our team specializes in fabricating custom splints to immobilize or support injured or weakened joints, as well as providing adaptive equipment to enhance daily living activities. We work closely with patients to assess their needs and design solutions that promote independence and comfort.

Pressure Garment and Scar Management

Specialized garments and techniques for managing scars and preventing complications after injury or surgery. Our pressure garment program is designed to minimize scarring, improve skin integrity, and enhance cosmetic outcomes. We provide education on scar management techniques, including massage therapy, silicone gel sheeting, and compression garments, to optimize healing and restore skin function.
